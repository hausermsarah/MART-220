Coffee Types

There are numerous types of coffee beverages enjoyed worldwide. Some popular varieties include:

  • Espresso

  • Cappuccino

  • Latte

  • Americano

  • Macchiato

  • Mocha

Each type has its own unique flavor and preparation method, making coffee a versatile and beloved drink.
